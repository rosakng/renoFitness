About Reno

I am Reno. I am a programmer in the day time. I was born in 70's. I am in the same boat like many other middle age and many other office professionals. I know how to train efficiantly in a busy life. I like to introduce many others people like me, with feasible and customized fitness solution, building up healthy life style.

I am AFLCA Certified Resistance Training Fitness Trainer. Certified Fitness Kickboxing Coach. I Help local Calgarian living in healthy and fitness life style since 2016.

I use weight training and fitness kickboxing training to help my clients consistently improve themselves in the long term general fitness journey. 

Whether your goal is losing weight, gaining muscle, eating healthier, or even all — I make sure you don’t stress the details and I have customized solutions that fit your needs.
