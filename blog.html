Resistance Training

By: Coach Reno

What are differences?

Weight Training, Resistance Training, Muscle Conditioning / Toning etc, they all are the same thing. But they have different workout planning on different purposes.
We often use different wieghts or band on the training.

  • Body Weight
  • Free Weights - Dumbbell, Barbell, Kettlebell etc.
  • Equipments - Smith Machine, Leg Curl Machine etc.
  • Resistance Bands
    • Preferably, more professional term is: Resistance Training.


    Somthing you should know about resistance training.

    'It' in this section all refer to Resistance Training.

  • It increasess lean muscle, muscle size and muscle strength while working against a weight or force
  • It increases bone density, keep your bone strong
  • It increases size and strength of tendons and ligaments
  • It decreases body fat
  • A beginner needs to train at least two or three times per week to gain the maximum benefit
  • Complete the Par-Q form and consult with professionals, such as your doctor, exercise physiologist, physiotherapist or registered exercise professional, before you start a new fitness program
  • Rest each muscle group for at least 48 hours to maximise gains in strength and size
  • Varying your workouts can help you push past a training plateau


    • A note from Reno:

    I recommend Resistance Training to the following people:

    1) If you are a beginner, want to know and learn more effective fitness training
    2) If you want to gain more muscle mass, no matter big size or muscle definition
    3) If you want to manage your weight
    4) If you want to learn more about nutrition intake and diet

    **RenoFitness Weight Training program can help with all the above tips.**

    Fitness Kickboxing

    By: Coach Reno

    What is Fitness Kickboxing?

    Fitness Kickboxing helps you reach your fitness goals through the kickboxing sports. Though kickboxing, is a competitive full contact amateur and professional sport, but, most people are attracted to fitness kickboxing for fitness benefits.

  • Learn self-defense
  • Cardiovarscular toning
  • Muscle conditioning
  • Motor skills enhancement


    • Somthing you should know about Fitness Kickboxing.

    RenoFitness Kickboxing class is a 60 mintues workout with trainees' safty as the primary goal. Class general contents includes:

  • Warm up
  • Techniques learning
  • Heavy Bag work
  • Partner Pad work
  • Muscle Conditioning
  • Strech and cool down


    • Fitness Kickboxing Equipment Guide

    Equipments include:

  • Gloves: 8-14 O.Z. for bag work. 16 O.Z. and above are must for sparring
  • Hand wraps: from 108"-180" in length, the longer the better
  • Shin Guards
  • Loose fitting shorts, T-shirt.
  • Head gear - optional for sparring
  • Mouth guard - optional for sparring


    • A note from Reno:

    I recommend Fitness Kickboxing to the following people:

    1) If you are a beginner want to know and learn Boxing, Kickboxing and MuayThai martial arts
    2) If you want to find a fun way to burn fat, instead of just running on treadmill
    3) If you want to learn self defense skills
